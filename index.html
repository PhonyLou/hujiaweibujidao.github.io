A Poem for Apple’s Steve Jobs

Author: Francis Morilao • Poetry


Five letter word starting with A,
Example of a fruit in the alphabet;
Everyone can easily guess,
First thing in mind you won’t regret.

Someone had a bite on that fruit,
And it became a big dispute;
When he invented a new way to compute,
Everyone started to salute.

Just recently we heard the news,
This man leaves us in blues;
All of them who follow his shoes,
Pay homage and amazing tributes.

So whenever you see an apple,
You will probably can recall;
The man who stood tall,
Steve Jobs leaves us an example.

He changed the way we think,
Improved the way we live;
After all of what he’s done,
Now, he is gone.

RIP Steve Jobs
1955 – 2011
